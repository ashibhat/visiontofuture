About Us

Our Story

Our founder Mark Smith was an employee of a small organisation for 5 years, when he realised that he hadn't been able to choose the skills got to focus on and develop while he was at the organisation.
That's when it clicked.
What if he could start an organisation that helped other organisations work hand-in-hand with their employees to develop career progression plans that benefited both the company and the employee.
And that is how the Vision and Value framework was born.
Vision and Value aims to equip small business owners to retain valuable employees and ensure that staff feel empowered to grow.
